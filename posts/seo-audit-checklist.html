SEO Audit Checklist (Starter)

A practical checklist to identify technical and on-page SEO issues and prioritise fixes.

Key points

  • Check indexing, sitemap, and robots configuration
  • Review Core Web Vitals and page speed bottlenecks
  • Fix duplicate titles, missing meta descriptions, and broken links
  • Optimise headings, internal linking, and content structure
  • Set up analytics and measure impact over time
