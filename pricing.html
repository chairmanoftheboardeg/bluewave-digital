Pricing

Every project is scoped—pricing depends on requirements and timeframe

We do not use a single fixed price for everything. Pricing is based on scope, complexity, deliverables, and deadlines. Below is a professional framework that explains how we estimate.

How we price projects

  • Scope: what is included and what is excluded.
  • Complexity: integrations, backend, database, or custom functionality.
  • Timeframe: standard vs accelerated delivery.
  • Support: one-time delivery vs ongoing optimisation/maintenance.
Important: all projects have different prices depending on what the client wants and their timeframe.

What you receive

  • Clear deliverables and timeline
  • Transparent communication
  • Performance and security considerations
  • Handover documentation (where applicable)

If you are unsure where to start, we can run a short discovery session and recommend the best approach.
